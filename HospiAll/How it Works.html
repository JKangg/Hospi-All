How it Works

Step 1:

Download the app and sign up

Once you have created an account, log in and you are ready to go.

Step 2:

Tell how us can we help you

Chosse from the wide range of health services available on our platform.

Step 3:

Schedule your consultation

Choose a time slot which is most convenient for you. You may choose your doctor from any of the government hospitals, polyclinics and participating GP clinics, or see a doctor recommended by us based on your symptoms.

Step 4:

Review the charges

Find out the consultation fees before proceeding with the appointment. Know exactly what you are paying for.

Step 5:

Consult your doctor

Begin the teleconsultation and reveive medical advice right away.

Step 6:

Recieve medication at your doorstep

We will deliver the medication to you right after the consultation.

Step 7:

See your consultation summary

Review your diagnosis, prescription, receipt and other medical documents right from the app. All your medical records will be securely stored in our database for you to keep track of your medical history.
