About us

Hospi-All, With You Every Step Of Your Recovery

As hospitals and specialist outpatient clinics gradually reopen to take in appointments, there is a new requirement to ensure safe distancing amongst patients waiting in the clinics. This significantly limits the maximum number of appointments that an outpatient clinic can arrange on any given day, resulting in long waiting time for specialist reviews. As a result, some patients run out of chronic medications and many others suffer the negative consequences of delayed diagnoses or management.

Our Mission

To deliver comprehensive and seamless integration of healthcare services so as to reduce strain on current hospital infrastructure.
